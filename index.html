FAST, SIMPLE AND WITH A GUARANTEED ANSWER

WHAT?

Specify what you want to do. There's a catch though, you can only use emojis.

WHERE?

Pick a location for your meetup from the map. This can be anything from a bar to a zoo.

WHEN?

Choose a time for your meetup. It can be anytime within the next 24 hours.

Make a plan by using the three different ingredients; emoji, location and time.

Send your invitation to one or several friends.

Your friends have 30 minutes to respond. If no answer is recieved, you'll have the option to send your invitation to someone else.
